‘conFUSEd FM’ is a comedy radio show, run by two morons who have absolutely no idea what they’re doing. Season 1 saw them journey from incompetent radio newbies, uncovering a plot against FUSE FM, culminating in a nail-biting face-off against the enemy forces of the tyrannical SU in an epic fight for the future of the station. This catapulted them to the dizzying heights of fame, with ConfusionMania sweeping the nation. With their sights set on Hollywood, the duo gambled their entire combined net worth on “CONFUSION: A Space Opera”, a star studded $1.3 billion space epic. After the complete and utter failure of this project (only 3 tickets sold), the duo came crawling back to the doors of FUSE FM – the only job that would accept them after this embarrassment. Washed up and $1.3 billion dollars in the hole, the second season has seen them attempt to claw their way back to credibility, with genre-hopping radio plays, a brand-spanking new website (it even has a fake merch store!) and a talking fax machine sidekick! But not all is as it seems at conFUSEd FM – the line between reality and fiction seems to be blurring, with the mysterious “C-Device” seemingly at the centre of it all. Tune into FUSE FM Mondays 8-9pm to see how this enthralling saga will conclude…
